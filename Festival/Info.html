Location

Below is a map of the place where the festival will take place, it is in the lush berewoods across an open green.

There are is a free bus ticket included in the ticket price to get you from the car park to the festival as enough parking isn't available.


what will you get when you buy a ticket?

Your ticket will include a pitch for over night camping, for an extra cost of £50 a night tepee can be hired out for a more luxury stay. A ticket purchase includes 3 alcoholic drinks and a meal at a stall of your choice.


contraband

No illegal drugs are to be permitted on the premises.

Fires are also not allowed unless contained in one of the burners which can be hired for £5 a night anyone caught starting fires outside a burner will be kicked from the premises.

Alcohol can only be consumed if purchased on site any found being brought in for outside will be confiscated.
